This project aims to analyze Ehsan platform data for the year 2024 through the development of an interactive dashboard using Power BI, with the objective of providing a clear and accurate overview of donation and campaign performance across different quarters.

The dashboard is based on data that includes total donations, number of campaigns created, number of operations, as well as the number and value of completed projects. The analysis focuses on the temporal distribution of donations across quarters, enabling the tracking of changes in donation volume over time, in addition to evaluating the platform’s outputs through completed projects and their value.

Campaign performance is also analyzed by classifying campaigns into those that exceeded the target, met the target, or fell below it, providing a clear indicator of success levels. The project further includes a metric to measure the achievement rate of campaign targets, reflecting the proportion of campaigns that met or exceeded their goals relative to the total number of campaigns created.

Data preprocessing and transformation were carried out using Power Query, while DAX measures were utilized to develop the required analytical calculations. The dashboard is designed with an emphasis on visual clarity and structured information presentation, enabling quick interpretation of key metrics and supporting performance monitoring as well as identifying gaps between actual results and targets over time.
